About me

My name is Anthony Lozano. I am from Danlí, Honduras, although I was born in the capital city, Tegucigalpa. I currently work for a company based in Arizona, United States, called Montessori Thrive. I work as a Client Manager, where I help and support Montessori schools. I have been married to my wife for four years, and we both love traveling and enjoying outdoor activities.

Danli, Honduras

Danlí is a city located in eastern Honduras, in the department of El Paraíso. It is known as “La Ciudad de las Colinas” because it is surrounded by hills and beautiful natural landscapes. Danlí has a pleasant climate and is famous for its tobacco production and high-quality cigars. The city is also recognized for its friendly people, rich culture, and strong connection to nature.
